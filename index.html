My Work

Autonomous Navigation for UGVs, UAVs, AMRs, etc.

Aid the UGVs (or AMRs) in unknown environments by avoiding obstacles by reacting to it very similar to how humans drive instead of using a pre-build map by adding landmarks and executing control plans. This approach does not rely on heavy and expensive sensors, rather simple yet effective stereo depth sensor(s), to generate depth map to be used in obstacle avoidance algorithm. The dependency towards AI is also eliminated by incorporating complex (and iterative) path-planning steps, instead a odometry (or GPS) controls is implemented to simply navigate towards the destination wile avoiding all the obstacles along the way. Works effectively in dynamic environments, as this method do not map the environment at everry step rather react.

  • Embedded Systems
  • IT: centralized networking
  • ROS2
  • Path-Planning
  • Computer Vision / OpenCV

Next Generation CT Injector

A full-fledged redesign of CT injector with all its components satisfying user needs of high flowrate. Major work on improving injector pump design design to a full spectrum of injection protocols from gender to cardiac output. Minimizing the risk of patient line failures by pre-stressing the tubes during high flow volume and flow-rate requirement for organs like heart and brain, where Contrast Medium attenuation peak is significantly narrow.

Breathe Analyzer Kit

A KWO complex based breath analyzer using ADC logic to map measured resistance to a diabetes result. Machine Learning and/or statistical modelling to correlate the KWO nanomaterial complex against human breath in terms of electrical signal to detect diabetes. An initial clinical test prototype was designed, with compact design for wide range of sensitivities using 3-D printing with PLA material, as a proof of concept.

Machine Learning Decision Tree Classification

A dataset consisting of all gilled mushrooms were to be identified with the toxicity levels. Information Theory (IT) was first applied to extract the important features having the most impact to toxicity, and then, a Decision Tree (DT) was implemented to prune and classify the attributes that led to the target variableL toxicity.
