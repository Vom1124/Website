Next Generation CT Injector

A full-fledged redesign of CT injector with all its components satisfying user needs of high flowrate. Major work on improving injector pump design design to a full spectrum of injection protocols from gender to cardiac output. Minimizing the risk of patient line failures by pre-stressing the tubes during high flow volume and flow-rate requirement for organs like heart and brain, where Contrast Medium attenuation peak is significantly narrow.

  • R&T Systems Level Analysis
  • CT Injector Design
